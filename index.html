About Every Market Media

Twenty+ years combined marketing data experience.

Every Market Media is a wholesale data compiler and data services consultant. We’ve used our over 20 years in database and email marketing to compile the highest quality and quantities available to resellers for release on the market today. Our services include complete file install licensing, direct list licensing for mid-size and enterprise end users, hygiene and verification for telephone/email data, data appends, data analysis and email marketing testing/management.

Modeling.We use high quality demographic data markers to help you figure out who and where your ideal customers are. This is often used in new acquisition testing for more rapid and accurate list refinement, making more prospects from a previously responsive group and correlating response data into actionable business intelligence for use in other marketing channels.

Email Append.Match email address to your file via a number of linking paths, including: postal address, phone, name, zip+4 and more. Individual, household and address level precision. Use to improve coverage on direct mail, renew unresponsive or bounced records from high value lists or refresh opt’d out records.

Data append and linking. Our three core databases are driven by hundreds of thousands of online attributes. Use these databases to establish empirical links between your own offline data, risk data, online display networks including Facebook and other popular DMP’s. Common applications include linking B2B to B2C records, analyzing response data, enhancing response data segments and adding additional marketing channels.

Brokerage.We receive new data from more than fifty suppliers, some feeding us new information as often as daily. Often many specific response files useful for specific applications are hidden gems in our supply chain. If you need specific response data EMM has the capability to look to its own supply chain and the management inventory of others. If we don’t have it in house, we’ll introduce you to the right person with the data. And if you’d like us to handle the transaction details we’re happy to do so for a standard fee of 20%.
